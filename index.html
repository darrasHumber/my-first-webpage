My Favorite Math Books

  1. Real Analysis by Walter Rudin
    A classic and rigorous introduction to real analysis.
  2. Linear Algebra Done Right by Sheldon Axler
    A clear and concise approach to linear algebra, focusing on vector spaces and linear maps.
  3. Topology by James Munkres
    The go-to textbook for understanding the fundamentals of topology.
